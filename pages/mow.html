About Project


MerryMeal is a charitable organization that prepares and delivers a hot noon meal to qualified adults living at home who are unable to cook or maintain their nutritional status due to age, disease, or disability.
Monday through Friday, the service will be available. Members who are not within a 10-kilometer radius of their outsourced kitchens and support will be provided with frozen meals over the weekend. MerryMeal has partnered with several food service providers across the country to provide the fastest possible delivery.


User Types Role based access
Member

Can register and login

Can request caregiver

Can order meals and get meals

Can Logout session
CareGiver

Can login and register

Can accept the requested member

Can view the meal system

Can cares for member

Can logout session
Admin

Can login to manage Meals on Wheels portal

Can check meals (Including accept, reject meals)

Can manage food safety (Including accept, reject meals)

Can manage user (View, Update and delete the user)

Can manage delivery (View delivery progress)

Can manage the fundraising (View total donate amount and view total expenses)

Can logout the session
Partner/ Volunteer

Can login and register

Can plan and prepare meal (Including view, edit and delete the meal. And also, they can submit (view, edit, delete and approve) the meals which is added from partner)

Can accept the member order and view details

Can view the accepted orders

Can logout the session
Donator/ Supporter

Can login and register

Can donate for meals (Including payment with PayPal)

Can received fund (Including view donator details and amount)

Can logout the session
Rider

Can login and register

Can deliver the meal

Can check the order (Including accept order)

Can view the order status

Can see the member address

Can logout the session
