About Project


Using the Spring MVC Framework and MySQL Server, create a website for a used vehicle sales portal. Users can update, delete, and upload car information in this portal, and administrators can also delete car information. The goal of this website is to connect buyers and sellers of automobiles.


User Types Role based access
User

Upload, delete and update their car information
Admin

Delete car information which is uploaded by every users.
